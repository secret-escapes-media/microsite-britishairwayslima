Trailblaze through epic landscapes

Peru is synonymous with adventurous spirit. From tackling the dense Amazon, bursting with back-to-nature thrills and stifling heat, simmer down trekking the glacial cool of Cordillera Blanca. The snow-capped mountain range offers the finest and most accessible glacial scenery in the world, perfect for trekking and mountain climbing expeditions. This also encapsulates the notion of Peru as a landscape of variety and activity; your next adventure starts here.

An extraordinary rail journey

Flying into Lima, you’ll be perfectly placed to board the recently restored train service to Huancayo – the world’s highest passenger railway and an engineering masterpiece. An unmissable experience, it climbs a breath-taking 4,829 metres in six hours, passing through a tapestry of green valleys, mining towns and glassy lakes.

Huacachina

A small, radiant jewel nestled in the golden sands of the desert, this veritable oasis is a sight to behold. The best vantage point, atop a towering sand dune, offers views of the palm tree encircled lagoon, spiralling out to a small collection of rustic hotels which are set to accommodate and to entertain any wearied traveller. With a host of sand-related fun to be had, from sandboarding down the huge dunes, or throttling around on a dune buggy, Huacachina is a charming paradise that is not to be missed.
