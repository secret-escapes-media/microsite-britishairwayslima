Explore Peru

From the remnants of ancient civilisations to Indiana Jones-style exploits in the rainforest, Peru catapults visitors into the spirit of adventure.

Some of the planet’s most awe-inspiring wonders are here – the life-sustaining Amazon, the sacred Machu Picchu and the highest tropical mountain range in the world, the Andes. And it’s a place that’s evolving rapidly. The capital, Lima, is a gastronomic powerhouse, home to some of the most innovative and exciting restaurants around. It feels about as far-flung as any holiday can get – so it might surprise you, then, that you can find direct British Airways flights from just £602* return from London Gatwick (no other airlines offer non-stop flights between London and Lima).
