Explore Peru

Dramatic landscapes fuse seamlessly with bustling cityscapes, in a country that invites spirited adventurers and intrepid explorers.

From the fringes of the Amazon, the heights of the Andes peaks and to the long beaches on the Pacific, Peru is a captivating hive of activity and leisurely pursuits. Unbeatable experiences, like trekking up to the enchanting Machu Picchu, or kicking back at the largest lake in South America, the beguiling Lake Titicaca, make Peru an awe-inspiring landscape where history and culture burst into action. Take Lima, the capital, where gastronomical refinement and authentic bites await in a myriad of high-end restaurants and propped up street food shacks, each offering a unique take on a diverse and sumptuous cuisine. If you can tear yourself away from the buzzing streets, Lima makes for a great platform to explore Peru’s many wonders, and with the only direct flights from the UK offered by British Airways from just £525 return* if you book before the 8th of November, this is the best time to explore this enthralling land.
